Spotify Wrapped Party 2023

Overview

We partnered with Spotify Korea to create their inaugural offline event, the 2023 Spotify Wrapped, held in Seoul. Our collaboration encompassed event curation, branding and design, as well as video and image production, to celebrate Spotify's year-end campaign 'Wrapped.' The event provided a vibrant setting for participants to engage in various activities, including an immersive zone dedicated to enjoying the Spotify Korea Editorial Playlist. The atmosphere was electrified by the dynamic sets of DJs Slom, while captivating performances by Hwang So-yoon, Paul Blanco, Zior Park and Dynamic Duo received a positive and enthusiastic response from all who attended.
